📘Guide (How to use this site) ♬

Keyboard Controls⌨

Use your computer keyboard to play:

  • Lower row (Z-M): C4 to B4
  • Upper row (Q-U): C5 to B5
  • Black keys: Numbers and letters above

Mouse Controls🖱️

Click or tap any key to play.

Recording🎤

Use Record button to start recording and Stop button to stop(the audio file of your recording will be automatically will be downloaded as WEBM File (.webm) ).....Also you can download your notes recorded as .json file by clicking the Download button!!
